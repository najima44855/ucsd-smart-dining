Smart Dining

Our Mission Goal

As high schoolers transition and ease into
collegiate life, they are faced with the challenge of
maintaining a balanced diet. This site is designed to help people
make smarter and healthier decisions with their diets.

Easy to Use

With just a few clicks and inputting your desired calorie
amount, you'll be able to find out the optimal meal to have at
each dining hall on campus! Whether you're an athlete, a health enthusiast,
or anyone that simply wants to improve their diet, the suggestions are compiled into this page for you!

Powerful Features

Our calorie tracker device allows you to check your
consumed amount of calories in a certain day! By automating the process
of tracking your intake amount, you'll be more motivated to stay on top of your diet game!
